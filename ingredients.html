Ingredients to make Apple Pie

  • Apples (Filling): 2 1/2 lbs of apples of your choice.
  • Sugar (Filling): 3/4 a cup of white granulated sugar (Brown Sugar is also a viable substitute).
  • Flour (Filling): Two tbs of white all-purpose flour.
  • Salt (Filling): 1/2 a tsp of salt of your choice.
  • cinnamon (Filling): 1 tsp of ground cinnamon.
  • Nutmeg (Filling): 1/4 a tsp of ground nutmeg.
  • Lemon (Filling): Juice of half a fresh lemon.
  • Egg (Eggwash): One egg, beaten.
  • Sugar (Crust): 1 tbs of white granulated sugar (Brown Sugar is also a viable substitute).
  • Flour (Crust): 2 1/2 cups of white all-purpose flour.
  • Salt (Crust): 1 tsp of a salt of your choice.
  • Butter (Crust): 1 1/2 sticks of cold, cubed butter.
  • Ice Water (Crust): 8 tbs of ice water or as needed to create a smooth dough.
