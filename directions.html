How to make an Apple Pie from scratch

Hope you enjoy!

  1. Start mixing dry ingredients: Mix flour and salt thoroughly in a medium-sized mixing bowl before adding cold cubed butter and ice water as needed.
  2. Prepare your dough: Once all of your ingredients are mixed in the bowl using your hands or a rolling pin kneed the dough until smooth and add flour as needed. The dough should not be sticky or tacky and once kneeded should be rolled into a ball, wrapped, and put in the refrigerator to rest.
  3. Start making your pie filling: Start by coring, peeling, and slicing apples of your choice and setting them aside. In a mixing bowl mix your flour, sugar, cinnamon, and nutmeg and mix with a fork or whisk. Once mixed incorporate or apples and begin to mix. Once mixed cut and squeeze the juice of half a lemon into the mixture and continue to mix until you feel the filling begin to thicken. Once done refrigerate the filling.
  4. Prepare your pie: Remove your dough from the fridge and cut it into two equal sized balls. Using your hands or a rolling pin spread and roll both balls of dough into sheets roughly a quarter of an inch thick. Once the sheets are finished drape one sheet of dough over a pan or pie tin of your choice. Once the first sheet of dough is set pour in your pie filling and drape the second sheet over top. using a fork press the edges of your crust and use a knife to cut vents and the excess dough from the edges. Beat two eggs and perform an eggwash over the pie and garnish with a dusting of sugar.
  5. Bake and Enjoy: Once your pie is ready place it in the oven at 375* for 50-60 minutes or until golden brown. Once finished remove the pie from the oven, let it rest for 10-15 minutes, and serve warm on its own or alongside your ice cream of choice. Enjoy!
